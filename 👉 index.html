About bol-ai

bol-ai is a high-performance reasoning engine conceptualized and architected by Vivek Dalvi. It is built to simulate advanced human-like logic with extreme speed.

The system runs on a custom HTML5/JS Core seamlessly integrated with Firebase Cloud Infrastructure. It is designed to handle complex queries, mathematical reasoning, and multi-lingual conversations without lag.

Version 1.1 introduces a new Voice Mode, persistent cloud settings, and fixes for local session management.

Logic Privacy Protocol

At bol-ai, privacy is not just a policy; it is part of the system architecture designed by Vivek Dalvi.

1. Data Sovereignty
Your conversations are yours. The chat history is stored locally on your physical device using Browser LocalStorage. We do not maintain a cloud database of your personal conversations.

2. Zero-Knowledge Analytics
Our servers only count the number of requests to ensure system stability. The actual content (the "payload") of your message is processed in real-time and then discarded from the server memory immediately after the response is generated.

3. Encryption Standards
All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3. This ensures that no third party can intercept the logic flow between your device and the bol-ai core.

We are committed to providing a secure, intelligent, and private AI experience.
